The Stalwart

Projects and experiments in language, markets, and culture.

I build small tools and datasets that try to measure things that are hard to measure — how people speak, how institutions communicate, and how tastes change over time.

Projects

  • Fedlock — scoring the orality of central bank speeches, from Greenspan-era formality to modern press-conference plain talk.
  • Havelock — a model that measures orality vs. literacy in written text.
  • haiku.zone — cross-category cultural recommendations: if you like this album, maybe you'd like this novel.
